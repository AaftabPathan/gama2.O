*Layers of the OSI Model*
There are 7 layers in the OSI Model and each layer has its specific role in handling data. All the layers are mentioned below:

1.Physical Layer
2.Data Link Layer
3.Network Layer
4.Transport Layer
5.Session Layer
6.Presentation Layer
7.Application Layer

SMTP, or Simple Mail Transfer Protocol,
 is the standard protocol for sending email messages over the internet.
  It acts as a delivery service,
   enabling email clients and servers to exchange messages.
    SMTP focuses on the transmission of emails from sender to recipient, and it doesn't handle the retrieval of emails.
